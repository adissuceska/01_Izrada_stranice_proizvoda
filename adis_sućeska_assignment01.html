Old Town Walking Tour in Sarajevo

70€ per person
★★★★☆ (4.5)

Discover the heart of Sarajevo on this immersive walking tour through the historic Old Town. Led by certified local guides, explore Ottoman architecture, hidden alleys, and vibrant markets.

Detailed Description

This 3-hour walking tour takes you through Sarajevo's most iconic Old Town neighborhood - Baščaršija. Marvel at the 500-year-old Gazi Husrev-beg Mosque, wander through copper workshops, and visit the famous Old Bridge where East meets West.

Our certified guides share personal stories and lesser-known facts that bring Sarajevo's complex history to life. Perfect for history lovers, culture enthusiasts, and first-time visitors.

Tour Specifications

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Group size: Maximum 15 people
  • Languages: English, Bosnian, German
  • Meeting point: Sebilj Fountain in Baščaršija
  • Level: Easy walking, cobblestone surfaces
  • Includes: Professional guide, map, water bottle
